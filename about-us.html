About GhanaTrust Bank

Your trusted partner in financial growth and security across Ghana

Our Story

Founded in 2006, GhanaTrust Bank has grown from a small community bank to one of Ghana's leading financial institutions. With over 20 years of experience in the Ghanaian banking sector, we have built our reputation on trust, security, and customer satisfaction.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible, reliable, and innovative banking solutions to all Ghanaians. Today, we serve over 500,000 customers through our network of 100+ branches nationwide and our comprehensive digital banking platform.

We are proud to be regulated by the Bank of Ghana and a member of the Ghana Association of Banks, ensuring that our customers' funds are protected and our operations meet the highest standards of banking excellence.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses across Ghana with innovative financial solutions that drive economic growth and financial inclusion, while maintaining the highest standards of security, integrity, and customer service.

Our Vision

To be Ghana's most trusted and innovative bank, recognized for excellence in customer service, technological advancement, and contribution to national development.
