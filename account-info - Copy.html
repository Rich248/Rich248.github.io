How to Open Your GhanaTrust Bank Account

Complete guide to opening your new bank account in just a few simple steps

Ready to Get Started?

Open Account Now

Step-by-Step Account Opening Process

1

Gather Required Documents

Before you begin, make sure you have all necessary documents ready. This will help streamline the application process.

  • Valid Ghana Card or government-issued photo ID
  • Ghana Card Number
  • Proof of address with digital address
  • Initial deposit information
2

Choose Your Account Type

Select the account that best fits your needs. We offer various options for different banking requirements.

Checking Account

Everyday banking with no minimum balance requirements

Savings Account

High-yield savings to grow your money faster

Business Account

Specialized accounts for business banking needs

3

Complete the Application

Fill out our secure online application form with your personal and contact information. The process typically takes 10-15 minutes.

Application Tips:

  • Double-check all information for accuracy
  • Have your documents ready for upload
  • Ensure your browser is updated for security
  • Use a secure internet connection
4

Identity Verification

We'll verify your identity using the documents you provide. This step ensures the security of your account and protects against fraud.

Verification Methods:

  • Document upload (Ghana Card, driver's license, passport)
  • Knowledge-based authentication questions
  • Address verification
  • Electronic signature
5

Initial Deposit

Make your initial deposit to activate your account. We offer multiple convenient funding options.

Funding Options:

  • Debit card transfer
  • Mobile money transfer (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Bank transfer from another bank
  • Cash deposit at any GhanaTrust Bank branch
6

Account Activation

Once verified and funded, your account will be activated immediately. You'll receive your account details via email.

What You'll Receive:

  • Account number and routing number
  • Online banking login credentials
  • Debit card ordering information
  • Welcome kit with account details

Account Requirements

18+ Years

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to open an individual account. Minors (under 18) can open accounts with a parent or legal guardian as a joint account holder.

Valid Identification

One of the following government-issued photo IDs:

  • Ghana Card
  • Driver's License
  • Passport
  • Voter's ID
  • NHIS Card

Ghana Card Number

A valid Ghana Card Number is required for account opening and tax reporting purposes.

Ghana Address

You must have a valid Ghanaian residential address with digital address. Proof of address may be required.

Initial Deposit

Minimum opening deposit requirements vary by account type:

  • Checking: GH₵50 minimum
  • Savings: GH₵200 minimum
  • Business: GH₵500 minimum

Credit History Check

We may review your banking history through credit bureaus. Previous negative banking history may affect account approval.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the account opening process take?

+

The online application typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. Most accounts are approved instantly, and you can start using your account the same day once you make your initial deposit.

What if I don't have a Ghana Card Number?

+

If you don't have a Ghana Card Number, you may be able to open an account with alternative identification options. Contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I open an account if I'm not a Ghanaian citizen?

+

Non-Ghanaian citizens can open accounts with valid identification and proof of address. Additional documentation may be required. Visit a branch location or call customer service for specific requirements.

Is my money safe at GhanaTrust Bank?

+

Yes! GhanaTrust Bank is regulated by the Bank of Ghana, which means your deposits are insured up to a certain amount. We also use advanced security measures to protect your information and funds.

What fees should I expect?

+

Many of our accounts have no monthly maintenance fees when you meet minimum balance requirements. We'll provide you with a complete fee schedule before you complete your application.
