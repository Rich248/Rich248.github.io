Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to open a checking account?

+

You'll need a valid Ghana Card, proof of address, and a recent utility bill. The process takes just a few minutes to complete.

Is there a minimum balance requirement?

+

No, our checking account has no minimum balance requirement. You can maintain any balance that works for your financial situation.

How long does it take to get my debit card?

+

Your debit card will be ready within 3-5 business days after account opening. You can collect it from any of our branches.

Can I link my mobile money account?

+

Yes! You can easily link your MTN, Vodafone, or AirtelTigo mobile money account for seamless transfers between services.
