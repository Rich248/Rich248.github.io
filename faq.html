Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What documents do I need to open an account? +

To open an account, you need a valid Ghana Card or Voter ID, your GPS Digital Address, and a passport-sized photograph. You'll also need to provide your personal information including name, phone number, and email address.

How long does it take to open an account? +

Our digital account opening process is quick and easy. Most applications are processed within 24 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email with your account details once approved.

What are the minimum balance requirements? +

Our Personal Checking account has GH¢0 minimum balance requirement. Personal Savings accounts earn competitive interest with no minimum balance. Business accounts have varying requirements based on the account type.

How do I link my mobile money account? +

You can link your MTN, Vodafone, or AirtelTigo mobile money account through our mobile banking app or by visiting any branch. This allows seamless transfers between your bank account and mobile money wallet.

What is the GPS Digital Address format? +

The GPS Digital Address format is GP-XXX-XXXX (e.g., GP-123-4567). This is Ghana's official addressing system used for accurate location identification.

How do I reset my banking password? +

You can reset your password through our mobile banking app using the "Forgot Password" option, or visit any branch with valid identification for assistance. For security reasons, password resets cannot be done over the phone.

Are my deposits insured? +

Yes, GhanaTrust Bank is regulated by the Bank of Ghana and your deposits are protected according to Ghanaian banking regulations. We maintain full compliance with all regulatory requirements.

How can I contact customer support? +

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@ghanatrustbank.com, call our hotline at +233 302 222 222, or visit any of our 20+ branch locations nationwide. Support is available 24/7 for urgent matters.
