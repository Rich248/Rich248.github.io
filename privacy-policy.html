Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Introduction

GhanaTrust Bank is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our banking services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, date of birth)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Financial information (account numbers, transaction history)
  • Government-issued identification (Ghana Card, Voter ID)
  • GPS Digital Address for branch services

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your account applications and transactions
  • To provide customer support and banking services
  • To comply with regulatory requirements (Bank of Ghana)
  • To enhance security and prevent fraud
  • To communicate important account information

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Your data is stored in compliance with Ghana's Data Protection Act.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@ghanatrustbank.com or visit any of our branch locations.
