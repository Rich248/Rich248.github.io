Security

Your security is our top priority

Our Security Commitment

GhanaTrust Bank employs industry-leading security measures to protect your accounts and personal information. We continuously monitor and update our security systems to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Encryption & Data Protection

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) protection for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive customer data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Fraud Prevention

  • Real-time transaction monitoring
  • Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
  • Automatic fraud detection and alert systems
  • 24/7 security operations center

Protecting Your Account

  • Never share your PIN or password with anyone
  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Monitor your account statements regularly
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep your contact information updated

Regulatory Compliance

GhanaTrust Bank is fully compliant with Bank of Ghana security regulations and international banking security standards including PCI DSS for payment card security.

Report Security Issues

If you suspect any security breach or unauthorized activity on your account, please contact us immediately at security@ghanatrustbank.com or call our 24/7 hotline at +233 800 353 4568.
