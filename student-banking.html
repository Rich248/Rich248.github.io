Student Account Benefits

GhanaTrust Bank offers specialized banking solutions for students in tertiary institutions across Ghana. Our student accounts are designed to help you manage your finances while focusing on your education.

No Monthly Fees

Student accounts have no monthly maintenance fees while you're enrolled in school.

Free Debit Card

Complimentary Visa debit card for purchases and ATM withdrawals nationwide.

Student Loans

Access to low-interest student loans for tuition and educational expenses.

Mobile Banking

Full digital banking access to manage your account from anywhere on campus.

Savings Tools

Automatic savings features to help you build financial discipline.

Financial Education

Free financial literacy resources and workshops for students.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be enrolled in a recognized tertiary institution in Ghana
  • Valid student ID or admission letter required
  • Ghana Card or Voter ID for identification
  • GPS Digital Address for account registration
  • Age 16-28 years old
